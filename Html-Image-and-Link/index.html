Who Am I ?

I am a European breed Doberman with ancestry from Germany. In the moonlight's gentle embrace, I stood tall and vigilant, With eyes that mirrored the stars, I watched over my domain, the very embodiment of loyalty and unwavering devotion. Each step I took resonated with the grace of a guardian, my heart beating in rhythm with the secrets of the shadows. In the quietude of the night, I was more than a dog; I was a protector, a confidant, and a silent companion to those who walked the paths of darkness.
